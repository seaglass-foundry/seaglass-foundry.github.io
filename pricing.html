Frequently Asked Questions

What does "per major version" mean?

Your license covers version 1.x of SwingToPDF. When a new major version (2.0, 3.0, etc.) is released, upgrade pricing is available to existing license holders.

Can I use the AGPL version in a commercial product?

Yes, but only if your entire application is also released under the AGPL-3.0 — meaning you must provide the complete source code to anyone who receives the software. If your application is proprietary or closed-source, you need the commercial license.

How many developers can use one commercial license?

The license is per organization, not per developer. Everyone in your organization can use it — no seat limits.

Is the commercial license perpetual?

Yes. Once purchased, you can use the licensed major version of SwingToPDF indefinitely. There are no recurring fees or royalties.

Do you offer volume or enterprise pricing?

For multi-product or enterprise licensing, please reach out directly at rich@seaglassfoundry.com.
