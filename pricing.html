Frequently Asked Questions

What does "per major version" mean?

Your license covers version 1.x of SwingToPDF, including all patch releases. When a new major version (2.0, 3.0, etc.) is released, existing customers receive upgrade pricing.

Can I use the AGPL version in a commercial product?

Yes, but only if your entire application is also released under the AGPL-3.0 — meaning you must provide the complete source code to anyone who receives the software. If your application is proprietary or closed-source, you need a commercial license.

What's the difference between Developer, Team, and Site?

Developer covers one named developer working on one project. Team covers up to five developers with unlimited projects. Site covers your entire organization with no limits on developers or projects.

Is the commercial license perpetual?

Yes. Once purchased, you can use the licensed major version of SwingToPDF indefinitely. There are no recurring fees or royalties.

How long will early adopter pricing last?

Early adopter pricing is available for a limited time. Lock in the 20% discount now — your license is perpetual, so the savings are permanent.

What if I need to redistribute SwingToPDF inside my product?

If you're embedding SwingToPDF in a product you distribute to other developers or organizations, you'll need an OEM license. Contact us for custom terms.
